What I Do

My genre of expertise in photography is mostly fashion landscapes because of the serene environment of the IIT Mandi campus and nearby places in Himachal Pradesh. Apart from these, I'm also interested in wildlife photography and planning to try it soon. I also do a little portrait photography which is mostly black and white.
In the videography field, along with travel vlogs, I've also worked on a bunch of short movies for which I thank Perception, the movie-making club of IIT Mandi. Some of the notable short films I've worked on under Perception are Elle, Sapne, The Link and Loser. Apart from all of this, I'm still curious about photography and keep learning new skills and techniques.

Who I Am

My name is Parimal Kumar and I'm 22 years old. I'm a final year student at Indian Institute of Technology Mandi and I'm originally from Patna, Bihar. I have been doing photography for 6 years but my interest boosted 4 years ago when I joined college. This was because of Shutterbugs, the photography club of IIT Mandi. It helped me pinpoint my passion for photography and work on it in the right direction.
I'm also a trekker and a travel vlogger for which I'm grateful to the beautiful Himalayan mountains of Himachal Pradesh.
